The OpenOffice.org Project



Preface

The purpose of this paper is to provide a strategic overview of the OpenOffice.org project. It is designed to deliver an introduction to the details of the principles and mechanisms involved in the operation project. Having a good understanding of these principles and mechanisms is key for anyone interested in becoming involved in the OpenOffice.org project.

It is expected that as the OpenOffice.org project matures, some of these details will evolve. This document will be updated periodically to reflect the nature of these changes to the strategies of the OpenOffice.org project.
