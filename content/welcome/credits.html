Apache OpenOffice Credits

OpenOffice is developed by a large community of volunteers who do the coding, testing, translation, documentation, support and many other project functions.
The Directory of Volunteers page gives credit to the many individuals who make OpenOffice possible.

Current Project Committers are listed on this Apache page.

We also have a large cumulative list of legacy contributors to OpenOffice.org 3.3.0 and before.

If you are interested in learning more about how to volunteer with Apache OpenOffice, you can read about it on our Get Involved page.
