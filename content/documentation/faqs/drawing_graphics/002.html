I am creating an organization chart. How do I evenly space my boxes?

Control over object spacing in a presentation or a drawing is called Distribution. Follow these steps:

  1. Select three or more drawing objects.
  2. Hold down the right mouse button to get the context menu.
  3. Select: Distribution.
  4. In the dialog that appears, you have the choice of aligning objects vertically and/or horizontally.
  5. Click on OK for your settings to take effect.
