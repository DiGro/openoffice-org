The mission of the Apache OpenOffice project is:

To create, as a community, the leading international office suite **that will run on all major platforms**.

The following platforms are supported for the most current release:

- Windows (XP, 2003, Vista, 7, 8, 10 and 11)
- GNU/Linux (x86 and x86-64, RPM and DEB based Linux distributions)
- Mac OS X (x86-64 only)

The [porting page][1] lists additional distributions that may be of interest.

To find out what you need to run the OpenOffice suite on your chosen platform, please click on one of the following links:

- [Apache OpenOffice 4.1.x][8]
- [Apache OpenOffice 4.0.x][2]
- [Apache OpenOffice 3.4.x][3]
- [OpenOffice.org 3.0 - 3.3][4]
- [OpenOffice.org 2.x][5]
- [OpenOffice.org 1.1][6]
- [OpenOffice.org 1.0][7]
