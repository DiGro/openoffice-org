Click on the Hosted Projects link on the left navigation bar. You will be taken to the Hosted Projects page, where all the project categories are listed along with the number of projects for each. Clicking on the category will take you to a list of all projects for that category. Once there, you can click on the title of the project to reach the project's home page.
